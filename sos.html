⚠️ Safety Tips for Tourists

  • 🔒Share your live location with a trusted contact when exploring alone.
  • 📱Keep phone above 30% — carry a power bank. SOS needs battery!
  • 👩Women: tap SOS or dial 1091 immediately if you feel unsafe.
  • 🗺️Stay in green (safe) geo-fence zones shown on the Live Map.
  • 🏥Note the nearest hospital address when checking into a new city.
  • 🚫Avoid isolated areas after 10 PM — stay in well-lit, busy streets.
  • 🚕Use only registered Ola/Uber — never unmarked vehicles.
  • 🤖AI monitoring is automatic — keep the app open while exploring.
